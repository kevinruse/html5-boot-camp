Grilled Avocado, Tomato & Feta Cheese Sandwich Recipe

Shopping List

  • 1 avocado
    • 2 medium tomatoes 4 ounces of feta cheese 8 slices of whole grain bread Olive oil

    Recipe

    Preparation Time: 15 minutes

    Pit and slice avocado. Thinly slice tomatoes and cheese. Layer avocado, tomatoes and cheese slices on bread. Pepper to taste and add second slice of bread. Repeat for each sandwich. Heat small amount of oil in non-stick pan. Grill on each side until bread is golden and cheese is slightly melted.
